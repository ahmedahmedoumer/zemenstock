Privacy Policy for Zemenstock

Last updated:

Introduction

Welcome to Zemenstock ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Zemenstock, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

Information We Collect

Zemenstock collects minimal information necessary to provide its core functionality. We collect the following types of information:

Camera Access

  • Purpose: The App requests camera permission exclusively for scanning QR codes and barcodes during the initial setup process.
  • Data Collected: No images, photos, or video recordings are stored, saved, or transmitted. Camera access is used only for real-time scanning and processing.
  • Storage: No camera data is stored on your device or our servers.
  • When Used: Camera access is only requested when you initiate the QR code scanning feature in the setup screen.

Location Access

  • Purpose: Location permission is required for Bluetooth device discovery and scanning, as Android requires location access to scan for nearby Bluetooth devices.
  • Data Collected: Approximate location information is used solely for Bluetooth device discovery purposes.
  • Storage: Location data is not stored, saved, or transmitted to our servers or third parties.
  • When Used: Location access is only used when searching for Bluetooth devices (such as thermal printers).

Bluetooth Access

  • Purpose: Bluetooth permission is required to connect to and communicate with thermal printers and other Bluetooth-enabled devices.
  • Data Collected: Bluetooth device names, MAC addresses, and connection status information.
  • Storage: Bluetooth device information is stored locally on your device only, to facilitate future connections. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • When Used: Bluetooth is accessed when you connect to or communicate with Bluetooth devices through the App.

Internet Access

  • Purpose: Required for app functionality, API communication, and syncing data with our servers.
  • Data Collected: Network requests and responses necessary for app operation.
  • Storage: Network data is processed in real-time and not stored beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

Push Notifications

  • Service Provider: OneSignal
  • Purpose: To send you push notifications and updates related to the App.
  • Data Collected: Device tokens and identifiers necessary for delivering push notifications.
  • Storage: Managed by OneSignal service. Please refer to OneSignal's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • To enable QR code and barcode scanning features
  • To facilitate Bluetooth device connections (primarily thermal printers)
  • To send you push notifications and important updates
  • To improve and optimize the App's performance
  • To ensure the security and integrity of the App

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • OneSignal: Device tokens and identifiers are shared with OneSignal for push notification services. OneSignal's data practices are governed by their own privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App, subject to confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Most data collected by the App is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Bluetooth device information is encrypted and stored securely on your device.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Camera data: Not retained (processed in real-time only)
  • Location data: Not retained (used only during Bluetooth scanning)
  • Bluetooth device information: Retained locally on your device until you delete the App or clear app data
  • Push notification tokens: Retained by OneSignal according to their retention policies

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have collected about you.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through your device settings.
  • Permission Control: You can revoke permissions (Camera, Location, Bluetooth) at any time through your device settings. Note that revoking permissions may limit App functionality.
  • Push Notifications: You can opt-out of push notifications through your device settings or within the App.
Note: Most data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data. For data managed by third-party services (such as OneSignal), please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
